About Me

I am currently the physician in charge at the Sexual Health Clinic and the Seri Support Centre, which is a care center for victims of sexual assault, both operating under Turku University Hospital, Finland. I also work as a private practitioner in obstetrics and gynecology, as well as a psychotherapist and sexual therapist. In addition to clinical work, I am actively involved in scientific research and supervise PhD and licentiate theses.
